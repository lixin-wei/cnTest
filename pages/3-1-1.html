小芳没把饼干送【 】小红。（xiao3fang1 mei2 ba3 bing3gan1 song4 【 】 xiao3hong2。）

Rule: When the result of the disposal or impact is that the thing is moved from one location to another location, we use the phrase “在 + new location + position word (such as 上, 左边)”. When the result of the disposal or impact is that the thing is transferred from one person to another person, we use the phrase “给 + new person”. When the result of the disposal or impact is that the thing is transformed from one thing to another thing, we use the phrase “成 + new thing”.
