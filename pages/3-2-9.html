Watch the video clip. Where should we put 在桌子上（zai4 zhuo1zi shang4）?

女孩把①闹钟②放③。（na3hai2 ba3 ① nao4zhong1 ② fang4 ③。）

Rule: When the result of the disposal or impact is that the thing is moved from one location to another location, we use the phrase “在 + new location + position word (such as 上, 左边)” AFTER the verb that describes the action.
