Watch the video clip. Where should we put 给小红（gei3 xiao3hong2）?

小芳把①可乐②递③。（xiao3fang1 ba3 ke3le4 ② di4 ③.）

Rule: When the result of the disposal or impact is that the thing is transferred from one person to another person, we use the phrase “给 + new person” AFTER the verb that describes the action.
