Introduction (PLEASE READ CAREFULLY)

This study aims to explore the effectiveness of some Chinese teaching and learning methods. Briefly speaking, you are mainly going to go through a learning tutorial, answer multiple choice questions, and make Chinese sentences. By completing these activities, you will learn some very useful Chinese words and sentences in an innovating way. This study consists of six sections, which are to be finished on two days: (1) Parts 1 to 4 on the first day (about 30-45 minutes), and (2) Parts 5 and 6 several days later (about 15 minutes). Below is a quick overview of what you are going to do in each section.

Part 1: You are going to familiarize yourself with some vocabulary, which will help you read or make sentences in the following sections. Note that in all following sections, you can always refer to the vocabulary sheet by clicking the grey part on the right side of the page.

Part 2: This section aims to see how much you know about the usage of Chinese ba (把) before going through the learning tutorial in Part 3. If you have little or no prior knowledge about ba (把), you can choose to skip this section and go directly to Part 3. Please tell the researcher via WeChat if you decide to skip this section.

Part 3: You are going to go through a learning tutorial and learn the usage of Chinese ba (把).

Part 4: This section aims to see how much you have learnt about the usage of ba (把).

Part 5: This section aims to see how much you still remember about the usage of ba (把) after several days.

Part 6. This directs you to a short questionnaire about your Chinese learning background.

Don’t hesitate to contact the researcher via WeChat if you encounter any questions while doing this study. Thank you very much!
