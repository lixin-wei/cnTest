Watch the video clip. Where should we put 成“大”字（cheng2 da4zi4）?

她把①“天”字②写③。（ta1 ba3 ① tian1zi4 ② xie3 ③。）

Rule: When the result of the disposal or impact is that the thing is transformed to or viewed as another thing, we use the phrase “成 + new thing” AFTER the verb that describes the action.
