Microphone Test

Please make sure that your microphone works and that your browser allows the use of microphone for recording. Speak to your microphone and see whether the visualizer on the top of the page (i.e., the black line) has any reaction. Please always check whether the visualizer has reaction as you speak when you go through the whole section.
